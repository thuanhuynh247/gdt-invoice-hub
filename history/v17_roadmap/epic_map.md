# Epic Map - Version 17.0.0: Statutory Accounting, Corporate Banking Reconciliation & Multi-Channel E-Commerce Tax Sync

## Feature Outcome
Establish a compliant, automated, and secure enterprise financial tax compliance and auditing workspace.

---

## Epics

### Statutory BCTC & Audit (E82)
- **Outcome**: Compile corporate financial statements and perform ledger integrity audits.
- **Complexity**: High

### Tax & Bank Reconciliation (E83)
- **Outcome**: Structure tax budget payment slips and reconcile invoices to bank statements.
- **Complexity**: Medium

### E-Commerce Sync & Match (E84)
- **Outcome**: Sync seller invoices and cross-check marketplace revenues with declarations.
- **Complexity**: Medium
