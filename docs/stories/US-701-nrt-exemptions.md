# US-701: NRT Exemption Auditor

## Story
**As a** tax compliance auditor,
**I want** to verify NRT exemptions for qualifying resource uses,
**So that** exempt extractions are correctly identified and zero-rated.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Agricultural/aquaculture/salt water → 100% exempt.
- Hydroelectric power generation water → 100% exempt.
- National defense/security resource extraction → 100% exempt.
- Exemption reason persisted in tenant database.
