# US-730: Environment Protection Fee for Wastewater Engine

## Story
**As a** compliance manager,
**I want** to calculate environmental protection fees for wastewater based on volume and pollutants,
**So that** I can ensure regulatory compliance under Decree 53/2020/NĐ-CP.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Calculate domestic wastewater fee (flat 10% of clean water purchasing price).
- Calculate industrial wastewater fee (flat base of 1,500,000 VND/year plus variable heavy-metal pollution factors: COD, TSS, Pb, Cd, Hg).
- Save fee calculations and parameters to the tenant database.
