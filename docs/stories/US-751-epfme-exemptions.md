# US-751: EPFME Exemption Auditor

## Story
**As a** tax auditor,
**I want** to identify mineral extraction activities that qualify for full exemption from environmental protection fees,
**So that** I can apply legal waivers and maintain accurate audit logs.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Support 100% fee exemption for household mineral extraction for personal construction within their land-use rights.
- Support 100% fee exemption for soil and stone extracted for security, military, or disaster prevention/relief works.
- Support 100% fee exemption for soil and waste returned to reclaim or restore the environment at the mining site under an approved plan.
- Log specific exemption reasons and support files in the audit history.
