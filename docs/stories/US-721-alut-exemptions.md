# US-721: ALUT Exemption Auditor

## Story
**As a** agricultural cooperative or household producer,
**I want** to audit my eligibility for agricultural land use tax exemptions,
**So that** I can apply waivers under Resolution 117/2020/QH14.

## Acceptance Criteria
- 100% exemption for households, individuals, agricultural cooperatives, and research/educational farms.
- 50% exemption for state-owned agricultural enterprises/organizations using land for non-exempt agricultural research.
- Non-exempt land (idle/speculative or non-agricultural use) gets 0% waiver.
- Persist exemption reasons and effective tax values to the tenant database.
