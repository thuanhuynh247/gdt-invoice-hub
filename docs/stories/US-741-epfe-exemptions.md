# US-741: EPFE Exemption Auditor

## Story
**As a** tax auditor,
**I want** to identify facilities and emission streams that qualify for full exemption from emissions fees,
**So that** I can avoid unnecessary compliance costs and maintain accurate audit logs.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Identify out-of-scope operations (small household businesses or facilities not requiring an environmental permit).
- Support zero-emission verified clean technologies for a 100% fee waiver.
- Log specific exemption reasons and support files in the audit history.
