# US-771: EPR Compliance Auditor and Exemption Inspector

## Story
**As a** compliance officer,
**I want** to audit EPR exemption qualifications,
**So that** I can apply relief under Decree 08/2022/NĐ-CP rules.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Support 100% EPR exemptions for:
  - Small scale revenue threshold: Annual revenue less than 30 billion VND.
  - Small scale import threshold: Annual import value less than 20 billion VND.
  - Export only: Products manufactured exclusively for export.
  - Closed-loop recycling: Certified closed-loop self-recycling system meeting or exceeding mandatory rates.
- Maintain audit logs in the tenant database detailing the exemption categories and reasons.
