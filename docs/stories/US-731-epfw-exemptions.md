# US-731: EPFW Exemption Auditor

## Story
**As an** environmental auditor,
**I want** to automatically verify environmental protection fee waivers for clean water and runoff,
**So that** we do not pay incorrect fees for exempted water operations.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Identify and waive fees for cooling water not in direct contact with pollutants (100% exempt).
- Identify and waive fees for natural rainwater/runoff water (100% exempt).
- Waive fees for domestic water consumed in rural agricultural households.
- Persist exemption reasons and calculations to the tenant database.
