# US-761: EPFSW Exemption Auditor

## Story
**As a** tax auditor,
**I want** to identify solid waste fee exemptions,
**So that** I can apply appropriate 100% relief rules under Decree 164/2016/NĐ-CP.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Support 100% solid waste fee exemptions for the following categories:
  - On-site self-recycling/reuse: Solid waste recycled or reused directly within the generating facility's production cycle.
  - Agricultural byproducts: Agricultural byproducts used in farming, livestock feed, or composting.
  - Domestic rural waste: Domestic waste from certified rural households.
- Maintain audit logs in the tenant database detailing the exemption categories and reasons.
